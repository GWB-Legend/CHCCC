ABOUT US

Excellence in Nursing Education

Founded in 1995, Christ Healing Community College has been Sierra Leone's premier nursing institution, dedicated to producing compassionate, highly skilled healthcare professionals who serve communities across West Africa and beyond.

Mission: To provide excellence in nursing education and compassionate patient care.

Vision: To be the leading nursing institution in West Africa.
